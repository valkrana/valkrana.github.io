Autograph

Valkrana fumbles multiple greetings, and your board. All that effort keeping it dry; now it's damp and dirty. She doesn't seem to care, just focuses on the lines to your sides. She regards your soiled board with contempt only as long as it takes to place her autograph. You shiver at that, too. Skeletons

A necromancer is never short on friends. She has them in spades.

Valkrana doesn't typically name her skeletons. Cute as they are, they are disposable tools. However, she will bestow a skeleton with a name if it performs a noteworthy feat.

Happy — Named as such because it looks happy. Survived an onslaught of umber hulks.

Pocky — Named as such because it is pock-marked. Survived a brush with the orange layer of a prismatic wall.
