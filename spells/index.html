Valkrana's Many-Tabbed Cookbook


The spells below were recovered from the remains of a waterlogged cookbook that once belonged to the awkward graftling necromancer, Valkrana Vil Galath Faeri Miraquinal.

Some notes reveal her enthusiasm for skeletons—that a skeleton hurled by a negative energy geyser is simply immensely cute. The aftermath is only a useful side effect. Others reveal a keen interest in intel gathering—that information is the most valuable commodity. Others, a penchant for defense—more specifically, an obsession with the well-being of those dear to her. All lead to the pursuit of a better, more fine-tuned control over both necromancy and the skeletons over which her necromancy grants dominion.

Valkrana's Dark-Aura Cookie

Necromancy cantrip
Casting Time: 1 action
Range: Self
Components: V, S, M (a cookie charm, bone meal)
Duration: Instantaneous

Choose a type of cookie that you have eaten in the past. You channel the spirit of the departed cookie to create in your palm a necromantic facsimile of a cookie of its type. The cookie, which exudes a deep dark aura, has no nutritional value. However, it is edible, and tastes like the cookie chosen at the time of casting.

Classes: Wizard
Subclasses: Arcane Trickster Rogue

Valkrana's Sacrificial Skelly

2nd-level necromancy
Casting Time: 1 reaction, which you take when a creature you can see hits you or a target you can see
Range: 120 feet
Components: V, S, M (a pock-marked hyoid bone)
Duration: Instantaneous

You will an undead creature within 5 feet of the target, and over which a class feature or necromancy spell has given you control, to block the attack, taking the attack’s damage in the place of the target creature.

Classes: Wizard
Subclasses: Arcane Trickster Rogue, Death Cleric, Divine Soul Sorcerer, Eldritch Knight Fighter, Oathbreaker Paladin, Spores Druid

Valkrana's Nuanced Command

2nd-level necromancy
Casting Time: 1 reaction, which you take when a creature you can see would provoke an Attack of Opportunity by you or an undead creature you control
Range: 120 feet
Components: V, S, M (a chevron bone)
Duration: Instantaneous

You may instead have any undead creature over which a class feature or necromancy spell has given you control, either directly or indirectly, make an attack on either the provoking creature or another target. This doesn’t use the creature’s reaction.

Classes: Wizard
Subclasses: Arcane Trickster Rogue, Death Cleric, Divine Soul Sorcerer, Eldritch Knight Fighter, Oathbreaker Paladin, Spores Druid

Valkrana's Rattling Mortar

3rd-level necromancy
Casting Time: 1 action
Range: 60 feet
Components: V, S, M (an undead creature under your control, which the spell destroys)
Duration: Instantaneous

Choose one undead creature within range over which a class feature or necromancy spell has given you control, either directly or indirectly, and a target square within 120 feet. A geyser of negative energy erupts beneath the creature, causing it to careen into the target square and explode in a 15-foot-radius sphere of negative energy and bones. Each creature in the sphere must make a Dexterity saving throw, or take 3d12 necrotic damage and 2d12 bludgeoning damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one.

Classes: Wizard
Subclasses: Arcane Trickster Rogue, Death Cleric, Divine Soul Sorcerer, Eldritch Knight Fighter, Oathbreaker Paladin, Spores Druid

Valkrana's Bone Disc

3rd-level necromancy
Casting Time: 1 action
Range: Touch
Components: V, S, M (a pouch of multifarious bones)
Duration: 8 hours

A disc of bones converges to float around the target, trailing wisps of negative energy. The bones are calm unless provoked by an incoming attack, at which point they form into a defense.

Whenever the target is attacked, roll a d4 and add the result to the target’s AC for the attack.

This spell ends once it has affected a number of attacks equal to 2 + your spellcasting modifier, minimum 2. The target can end the spell early by using an action to dismiss it.

Classes: Wizard
Subclasses: Arcane Trickster Rogue, Death Cleric, Divine Soul Sorcerer, Eldritch Knight Fighter, Oathbreaker Paladin, Spores Druid

Valkrana's Lesser Skeleport

4th-level necromancy
Casting Time: 1 action
Range: Self
Components: V
Duration: Concentration, up to 1 minute

You swap skeletons; then, a second later, the rest. When you cast this spell, and at the end of each of your turns before the spell ends, you can choose to trade places with one undead creature over which a class feature or necromancy spell has given you control, either directly or indirectly, and which is within 120 feet. Until the end of your next turn, the undead creature with which you have traded places gains temporary hit points equal to your character level.

Classes: Wizard
Subclasses: Arcane Trickster Rogue, Death Cleric, Divine Soul Sorcerer, Eldritch Knight Fighter, Oathbreaker Paladin, Spores Druid

Valkrana's Enduring Link

5th-level necromancy
Casting Time: 1 minute
Range: Self
Components: V, S, M (a charred scapula incised with eyes)
Duration: Concentration, up to 10 minutes

Your necromancy leaves a lasting impression. Choose one undead creature over which a class feature or necromancy spell has ever given you control, either directly or indirectly, which has an Intelligence of 11 or lower. If you no longer control the creature, it must fail on a Charisma saving throw against your spell save DC in order for this spell to succeed on that creature. If the target creature is on a different plane than you, there is a 5% chance the spell fails.

For the duration of the spell, you both control the creature (issuing mental commands with no action required by you) and share its senses, gaining the benefits of any special senses it has when perceiving through its senses.

Classes: Wizard
Subclasses: Arcane Trickster Rogue, Death Cleric, Divine Soul Sorcerer, Eldritch Knight Fighter, Oathbreaker Paladin, Spores Druid

Valkrana's Greater Skeleport

8th-level necromancy
Casting Time: 1 action
Range: Self
Components: V, S, M
Duration: Concentration, up to 10 minutes

You swap skeletons; then, a second later, the rest. When you cast this spell, and at the end of each of your turns before the spell ends, you can choose to trade places with one undead creature over which a class feature or necromancy spell has ever given you control, either directly or indirectly, and which has an Intelligence of 11 or lower. If the target creature is on a different plane than you, there is a 5% chance the swap fails, but this does not cause the spell to end prematurely.

When swapping in this manner, you can choose to bring with you a number of willing target creatures up to your spellcasting modifier, and can change these targets with each subsequent swap.

Classes: Wizard
Subclasses: Arcane Trickster Rogue, Death Cleric, Divine Soul Sorcerer, Eldritch Knight Fighter, Oathbreaker Paladin, Spores Druid
